About

I'm a Software Engineer, where I solve business and production problems using C#, C++, and occasionally Python. I'm also a Master's candidate in Computer Science at the University of Pennsylvania: School of Engineering and Applied Science.

I'm a huge OSS proponent and a have contributed to several projects. I also own and maintain two projects: Linux terminal program vizex and a fuzzer (random testing for C++ projects).

The vizex visually displays disk usage, directory data, directory tree within a terminal. It's like a combination of popular Linux commands du, df, ls but on steroids. Previously he worked as a Data Scientist.

I come to this blog and I log about various topics I learned and problems I solved. Sometimes I even get Philosophical and try to see computers from metaphysical perspective.

I love everything Sci-fi and consider myself a Lego enthusiast. My interests and career goals are aligned with Software Engineering. I'm passionate about better understanding computers and using this knowledge to solve real-world problems.
